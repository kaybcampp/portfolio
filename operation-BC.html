Problem

The client, a small business owner, needed a fully functional marketplace that could be accessed via Telegram, with an intuitive shopping experience for customers. The bot required a comprehensive menu, organized product categories, add-to-cart functionality, and payment integration.

Solution

To meet these needs, I developed a Telegram bot using Python and the Telegram API. The bot features a welcoming interface with a message guiding users through its functionality. Products are categorized for easy navigation, with detailed descriptions and prices displayed. The add-to-cart function accurately updates item quantities and the total balance, creating a seamless shopping experience.

What's Next

The project awaits the client's company logos and their preferred payment integration method to finalize the shopping and checkout experience.
